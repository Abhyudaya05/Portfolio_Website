Who Am I?

I'm a B.Tech Computer Science student specializing in Data Science & Analytics at JECRC University, Jaipur — 3rd year in, GPA of 8.78, and genuinely obsessed with the moment a messy dataset suddenly makes sense. Give me noisy data and a question that matters — I'll find the signal, build the dashboard, and make the model generalise.

I've worked hands-on with Python, SQL, Power BI, and statistical analysis — not just academically, but on real problems: customer segmentation that actually informed targeting, sales forecasting with measurable accuracy, and churn prediction pipelines built end-to-end. Clean pipelines, honest analysis, outputs that non-technical people can act on — that's the bar I hold myself to.

When I'm not in the IDE — I'm on the ground as an NSS volunteer, behind the scenes at the Jaipur Literature Festival, or in the room at diplomacy summits. I show up. Every time.
